Our Services

Realistic virtual airline services for members and community.

Fleet Ops & Route Network

Structured routes across our hubs and network.

We maintain a curated network of routes optimised for our fleet mix ‚Äî A320neo, Embraer E190, ATR72/42. Members get access to preferred routes, recommended procedures and event routes for community fly-ins.

Pilot Training & Mentoring

Structured mentoring and training programs.

We provide mentoring, procedural training, and event coaching to help pilots improve technique, learn realistic approaches (including ILS procedures) and become active community members.

Community Events

Regular fly-ins, group flights and competitions.

Our events calendar features weekly group flights, seasonal events and challenges. Staff-led events include briefings, route packs and ATC coordination where possible.

Guides & Resources

Checklists, how-tos and pilot resources.

Downloadable guides, recommended procedure notes and checklists to support safe and realistic operations. Content is optimised for popular aircraft used by members.
